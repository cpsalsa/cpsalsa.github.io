**Bachata:** 7:00 - 8:00 PM<br>
**Salsa:** - 8:00 - 9:30 PM<br>
**Social Dancing Starts:** - 9:30 PM

## Cost
**$8** per lesson (social dancing included)<br>
**$5** if only attending social dancing
