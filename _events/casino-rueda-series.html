Salsa Rueda de Casino

When you see a group of dancers dancing salsa together and exchanging partners like a square dance, you're seeing a form of salsa known as Casino Rueda. Typically, it consists of two or more partners, one of which is the caller who calls out the specific pattern, all of this danced in a circle (Rueda means wheel).

Want to learn it?

If you want to learn it, you're in luck! CPSalsa is hosting a 5-week progressive workshop series on Casino Rueda. This series is ideal for beginning and intermediate dancers.

Each week will introduce new concepts and moves designed to challenge and grow your dancing, and since it's progressive, will build on what was learned the previous week.
